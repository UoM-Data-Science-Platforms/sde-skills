    Our Project

    The future of secure research depends on skilled, knowledgeable, and experienced colleagues who can navigate complex technical and governance landscapes. In addition to defining core competencies at entry, mid, and senior levels for existing colleagues to help them to navigate and plan their career progression, we're developing the next generation of secure data environment professionals through hands-on projects, structured mentoring, and bespoke training and development plans. Discover how we're using insights from our competency framework to guide our students and trainees, and how their real-world experiences are feeding back into the framework to ensure it remains grounded in practice.

    Student Projects

    To ensure future sustainability for the technical workforce, we are supervising students from Masters degree programmes at the three host institutions. Over two academic years, we have proposed research and technical projects with real-world usecases from Secure Data Environments in the North West of England. In addition, all of our research team provide mentoring to students to help them to develop technical/professional skills and to discover formal training opportunities that will help them to standout in the jobs market post-graduation, and to inspire them to pursue careers designing, developing, and operating SDEs.     SDE RTP Competency Framework

    We have developed a comprehensive competency framework alongside a growing community of Trusted Research Environment and Secure Data Environment developers and operators. The framework maps core skills, experience, tools, and technologies to the Five Safes pillars and SATRE requirements, enabling service providers to grow capability whilst expanding technical teams, and to empower practitioners to plan for their professional progression. By grounding the framework in real-world practice and test-driving it in real SDE development teams, we are helping to ensure the sustainability and excellence of UK secure data environment programmes.

    Key Features

    Six Core Domains

    Comprehensive coverage of Safe Access & Identity, Data Management, Governance & Compliance, and more.

    Competency Mapping

    Detailed competency levels with proficiency descriptions for career development.

    SATRE Aligned

    Mapped to UK research ethics frameworks for consistent professional standards.

    Collaborative Design

    Developed by teams across Manchester, Liverpool, and Lancashire institutions.
